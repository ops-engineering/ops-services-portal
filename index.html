About

For well over a decade we have led infrastructure and DevOps initiatives through the real work of keeping platforms stable while shipping faster — balancing automation, security, and cost without losing sight of the business outcome.

Our expertise spans Kubernetes and OpenShift, GitOps-style delivery, Jenkins and pipeline-as-code and full-stack observability with Prometheus and the Grafana ecosystem. We have operated Kafka and streaming stacks at scale on AWS and hybrid estates, and shipped production CI/CD with Terraform, Ansible, and the glue code that makes it repeatable.

We partner closely with product and engineering, own problems end to end, and help teams build platforms they can run with — not black boxes that only one person understands.
